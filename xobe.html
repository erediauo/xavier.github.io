xobe

xobe is a London-based digital artist working through highly conceptual and empathetic approaches to image-making.

Their practice is guided by faith, spirituality, and a curiosity around the spiritual self. Working intuitively, pieces form through subtle shifts in energy, environment, and feeling.

Influenced by Gustave Doré, Jean-Michel Basquiat, and Asako Narahashi, their work blends metaphor, surrealism, and emotional tension to explore belief, liminality, and the human condition.

INTERFERENCE

The aim of this project was to interfere with visual perception, encouraging viewers to rely on memory and recognition to fill in gaps.

These pieces are not complete without audience participation. Through engagement, familiar symbols emerge in unfamiliar ways.

INTERFERENCE explores uncanny familiarity and challenges the objectivity of visual language by revealing its emotional and psychological depth.
